    Chord D

    For chord D your first finger needs to be on string G, your second finger should be on high E and your third finger will need to be on string B. The first fret is where your first and second fingers should be and your third finger should be placed on the third fret.

  • Chord F
    Chord F

    The F chord is another cord that will require you to use four fingers. Your first finger should be placed on string B, your second finger on string G, third finger on A and your fourth finger on string D. The third and fourth finger is placed on the third fret, second finger on the second fret and the first finger on the first fret.
