    Chord A5

    For this chord there are three variations methods of playing it. The first image requires you to have your first finger on string E on the fifth fret and your second finger on string A on the seventh fret.
    The second image only requires on efinger and that will need to be placed the D string on the second fret.
    The final image shows that your first finger needs to be on the D string on the seventh fret and your second finger on string G on the ninth fret.
